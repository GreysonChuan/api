SALE

  • Request Parameters
No Name Type Length Description/Value
1 return_url M Alphanumeric Your app URL Scheme
2 pwd M Alphanumeric Password for FDMS mPOS
3 return_key M Alphanumeric Unique key for order identification
4 type M Alphabet 1 S : sale
5 amount M Numeric 10 For sale / Except GST and Service Charge amount
6 tip_amount O Numeric 10 Tip amount
7 reference_no O Alphanumeric Reference No. in mPOS app
8 any others O Any other key & value pairs to return
  • Response Parameters
No Name Type Length Description/Value
1 return_key M Alphanumeric Unique key for order identification
2 result_code M Alphabet 4 0000 : succeed / others: failed
3 result_message M Alphabet SUCCEED / Failed message
4 type M Alphabet 1 S : sale
5 receipt_no M Alphanumeric 14
6 sales_amount M Numeric
7 tip_amount M Numeric 10
8 gst_amount M Numeric 10
